ABOUT THE VESSEL

Peregrine is a Bruce Roberts Voyager 495, a 50-foot steel expedition-style sailing vessel built for strength, reliability, and long-distance exploration. Designed with safety and comfort in mind, she is the perfect liveaboard or bluewater cruiser, ideal for ocean passages or extended coastal adventures.
